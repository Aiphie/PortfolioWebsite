LOGO DESIGNS

BCBP Youth Binan

Made in 2019. BCBP Youth Binan is a Catholic youth group in which I am a member of and I was asked to create our logo. Since it is a sub-group of a larger community, I integrated the main BCBP logo in the 'o' of "youth". The colors complement our motto which is "From the rising of the sun to its setting, praised be the name of the Lord" the latter part of the motto is translated into Latin "Laudabile nomen Domini".

One Farmvest

Made in 2022. One Farmvest is a company that aims to provide fresh produce at fair prices for both consumers and farmers. They requested for a simple yet recognizable logo.

Learne Ventures Inc.

Made in 2017. Learne Ventures was another startup company that requested for a monogram-type of logo.

Chariot Games

Made in 2023. Chariot Games is a fictional game studio made for our school project. The logo is a combination of a game controller and a horse utilizing negative space for some of its details.

Sophia Bianca

Made in 2022. This is my logo. Taking my initials S and B and creating a unique monogram combining the two letters.

Class Logo for Hoodie

Made in 2019. This logo was made for my 10th grade class called Saint Septimus to be put as a design for our custom hoodies to serve as souvenirs for our last year in junior high school. The lightning resembles the letter S and symbolizes our most memorable class performance: a musical about the Greek gods and goddesses.

MPT House of Jewelry

Made in 2023. MPT House of Jewelry is a small business based in the USA but is owned by Filipinos. I sketched different designs and asked for their thoughts since they didn't have an idea of what they want at first. They then showed me one of their earrings that contain symbols from the Philippine flag and told me they want a logo with it. I then proceeded to vectorize the design.

Crafts and Caboodles

Made in 2021. Crafts and Caboodles is another small business selling cute yet elegant looking jewelries and pendants made from polymer clay. Keeping that in mind, I made a logo that radiates creativity and elegance.

MOVIE POSTER AND BOOK COVER

Made in 2018. This was another school project where we were tasked to create a movie poster and a short film about the story of Ceyx and Alcyone. We photographed our classmates in a stairwell to mimic the effect of sitting on a rock. I edited them out of the original photo and into this rocky beach scene and added the two king fisher birds in relation to the story. The challenge here was to match the lighting so that they appear to be part of their environment.

Made in 2018. This book cover is for a story with the themes of romance and mystery. The illustrations are all digitally drawn by myself and edited the male lead to look glitched.

Made in 2019. This is a work of image manipulation. I took parts of many different photos to make the forest. ANIMATION AND MOTION GRAPHICS

BACK TO NORMAL

Back to Normal was part of my OJT project in senior high school. The whole project consists of a website, an animation, and an intercative informational "game" about Covid19. My main role in this project was to be the team leader. Aside from leading the group, I also took part in the whole process of the project development especially in animation. You may view our website through this link: Back to Normal.

ANQUA

Anqua was also one of my team projects in senior high school for our animation class. It is a game made in Adobe Animate and uses ActionScript 3. The goal in this game is to reach the finish line without bumping into the obstacles. This features increasing speeds as the game progresses.

IMC Tool Cards

We were tasked to creatively present the Integrated Marketing Communication Tools for our Advertising Practice course in my one year in BFA Advertising Arts. Using Adobe After Effects, I made our static game-card-styled work into an animated 3D deck.

3D Logo Animation

I made this logo and animation for the organization I am in. Also using Adobe After Effects, I made our logo rotate and have some shine to be added in our audio visual presentations and introductions.
